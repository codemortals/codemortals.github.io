Mentoring

We work with many individuals both junior and senior, giving them an opportunity to talk about their work or even extra curricular activities. This open and honest forum helps your team members to have an independent person to build a relationship with. Those involved in mentoring programs have the opportunity to attend events and get 1 to 1 advice from specialists in their field.

The most effective form of learning is to have an ongoing relationship. Just consider through your career how often you would like to have had someone to discuss a problem with, bouncing ideas or to just confide in. Our mentors take confidentiality very seriously too so you can be sure your team feel comfortable in this safe environment and working towards a stronger culture.

A mentor is someone who focuses on development not just performance.

Skills gap analysis

As industry experts we are able to review individuals, teams and departments to understand their strengths and weaknesses. The first step towards improving efficiency is to understand the lay of the land, we do this through 1 to 1 interviews and monitoring of daily activities.

Once we have completed a short period of discovery and retrospective, analysis gaps can be identified and remediation can be recommended. This could be through training, re-structuring or recruitment; depending on the most cost effective strategy and future growth plans.
